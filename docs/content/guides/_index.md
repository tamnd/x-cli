Each guide is built around a job rather than a command: reading tweets and
profiles, searching and finding people, walking the graph linked from a tweet or
account, working from your own account, shaping and piping the output, and
keeping a local copy in SQLite. They assume you have run the
[quick start](/getting-started/quick-start/).
